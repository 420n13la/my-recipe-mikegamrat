Pierogies recipe

What is a pierogi?

A small dough dumpling stuffed with a filling such as potato or cheese, typically served as a dish with onions or sour cream. This recipe is for 16 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • Half a gallon of milk
  • 500ml of sour cream
  • a lot of love

Steps

  1. Heat oven at 5778 K;
  2. Beat the egg
  3. Drink half a gallon and add the other gallon of milk
  4. Make sure the egg is beat
  5. Put stuff in the oven
  6. Run a marathon and come back
